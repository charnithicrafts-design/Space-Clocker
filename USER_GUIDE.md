# 🚀 Space-Clocker: Mission Commander's Guide

Welcome to **Space-Clocker**, your elite command center for navigating the trajectory from ambitious goals to daily execution. Whether you are aiming for ISRO, NASA, or mastering complex technical fields, this guide will help you pilot your productivity with precision.

## 🌌 Core Philosophy: The Momentum Loop
Space-Clocker is built on the principle of **Reflecting Momentum**. It connects your high-level **Ambitions** (The Nebula) to your daily **Protocols** (The Orbit), ensuring every "small step" contributes to your "giant leap."

---

## 🛠 1. The Momentum Engine (Dashboard)
Your primary flight deck. This screen provides a high-level overview of your current status.

- **Orbit Level:** Your progress is gamified. Complete tasks and milestones to gain XP and increase your Orbit Level.
- **Resonance Progress:** Watch your energy build. The resonance bar indicates how close you are to your next level-up (1,000 XP per level).
- **Macro Ambitions:** A summary of your active trajectories. Each card shows a real-time progress percentage linked to its underlying milestones.
- **Real-time XP:** Task completion immediately injects XP into your resonance bar. Important milestones yield higher "Resonance Weightage" (XP).

---

## 🏗 2. The Nebula Map (Architect Mode)
The "Architect Mode" is where you deconstruct the complex into the actionable.

- **Launch Trajectories:** Click **LAUNCH TRAJECTORY** to define a new high-level ambition (e.g., "Master Quantum-ML").
- **Deconstruct into Milestones:** Break your ambition into manageable phases. Each trajectory can host multiple milestones.
- **Task Splitting:** Within each milestone, use **SPLIT INTO NEW TASK** to create specific sub-goals. 
- **Milestone Weightage:** Completing a task within a milestone grants **25 XP**, while standalone daily tasks grant **10 XP**.

---

## 🛰 3. Orbit (Daily Protocol)
Your daily mission log. This is where the actual "work" happens.

### 🌓 UI Strategy Modes
Configure your interface in **Settings** to match your experience level:
- **Simple Mode:** Focuses on task titles and entry times. Ideal for quick task management.
- **Professional Mode:** Unlocks advanced mission parameters including **Descent Completion** (End Time), **Deadlines**, and mandatory **Neural Reflections**.

### 📅 Task Scheduler
- **Entry Time:** Plan when a task enters your orbit.
- **Descent Completion (Pro):** Set a target time for task completion to visualize your "Descent Trajectory."
- **Deadlines (Pro):** Assign strict timestamps for critical mission parameters.

### 🧠 Stellar Scheduler (Intelligent Conflict Detection)
A centralized intelligence layer monitors your mission log for anomalies:
- **Temporal Overlaps:** Detects if a task's "Descent" overlaps with the "Entry" of another.
- **Orbital Density:** Warns you if more than 8 active tasks are scheduled, indicating high system pressure (>85%).
- **Atmospheric Entry:** Alerts you when a deadline is less than 4 hours away.
- **Breach Alerts:** Notifies you immediately if a deadline window has closed.

---

## 🧘 4. Reflection Loop
If you fall out of sync or engage with The Void, the system encourages recalibration.

- **Neural Reflections (Pro):** In Professional Mode, completing a task or clicking the **Neural Reflection** button opens a debriefing modal.
- **Trajectory Analysis:** Use this to analyze missed parameters or void engagements.
- **Mindful Recalibration:** These entries are saved as "Reflection Swells," helping you identify patterns and adjust your trajectories over time.

---

## 🌑 5. The Void Protocol (Anti-Habits)
Productivity isn't just about what you do; it's about what you *don't* do.

- **The Not-To-Do List:** Tracks anti-habits (e.g., "Doomscrolling," "Unplanned Debugging").
- **Visual Friction:** Engagement with the Void is marked with red alerts and low-frequency "Thud" sounds to discourage these behaviors.

---

## 📊 6. The Skills Matrix
A technical radar for tracking your proficiency across different domains.

- **Radar Visualization:** View your skills on a multi-axis chart.
- **Current vs. Target:** Toggle between your current level and your target proficiency to visualize the "Skill Gap."
- **Gap Analysis:** Each skill card provides a percentage-based breakdown and AI-driven recommendations on how to close the gap.

---

## 🔊 7. Integrated Audio Feedback
The **SoundManager** provides organic feedback to reward your dopamine receptors:
- **"Swell":** Triggers when launching a new Trajectory or restoring a snapshot (Expansion).
- **"Pop":** A crisp sound for task completion, additions, or navigation (Satisfaction).
- **"Thud":** A low warning for deletions, Void engagement, or failed uplinks (Caution).
- **"Uplink":** A high-tech transmission sound when starting a cloud sync or snapshot creation.
- **"Success Arpeggio":** A melodic tone indicating a successful telemetry sync or data restore.

---

## 📡 8. The Communication Array (Data Portability)
Protect your trajectory data and maintain continuity across multiple command centers.

### Chronos Backup (Binary Snapshots)
- **Binary Precision:** capture the entire physical state of your local database (`.pgdump`).
- **Create Snapshot:** Generate a timestamped recovery point. 
- **System Restore:** Hot-swap your current database with a previous snapshot.

### Stellar Sync (Cloud Uplink)
- **Neural Link:** Connect your command center to **Google Drive**.
- **Telemetry Uplink:** Manually push your latest trajectory to the Nebula.
- **Divergence Alerts:** The system detects **Temporal Rifts** if cloud data is newer than local data.
