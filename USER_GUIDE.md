# Space-Clocker User Guide

Welcome, Professional. Space-Clocker is your high-fidelity Career Hub for tracking trajectories toward AWS Solutions Architect, Data Analyst, and beyond.

## Core Sectors

### 1. Momentum (Dashboard)
Your high-level overview.
- **Career Resonance:** Your global progress across all trajectories.
- **Daily Trajectory:** Quick look at today's critical objectives.

### 2. Nebula (Architect Mode)
Decompose your ambitions into progression milestones.
- **Trajectories:** Main goals (e.g., "AWS Solutions Architect Professional").
- **Milestones:** Significant steps within a trajectory.
- **Optimal Tasks:** Granular actions linked to milestones.

### 3. Orbit (Daily Scheduler)
Manage your daily mission logs.
- **Tactical View:** Filter by Daily, Weekly, or Yearly horizons.
- **Critical Gravity:** Automated alerts for approaching deadlines.
- **The Void Protocol:** Track activities to avoid (Not-To-Do list). Engaging with these impacts your professional reliability.

### 4. Progression Map (Chronometer)
Visualize your journey through time.
- **Daily View:** Standard calendar for granular planning.
- **Weekly View:** Visualizes 4-week bars of the current month to track sustained momentum.
- **Yearly View:** A 3-year trajectory (Previous, Current, Next) highlighting long-term alignment, specifically targeting the **AWS Professional** milestone.

### 5. Transmission (Reporting)
Generate career briefings for self-reflection or professional submission.
- **Trajectory Chronometer:** Select a specific date range to analyze.
- **Horizon Grouping:** Achievements are automatically grouped by Daily, Weekly, and Yearly horizons.
- **Architect Mode Progress:** Includes completed milestones and overall trajectory alignment.
- **Trajectory Alignment:** Automated detection of alignment with major goals like **AWS Solutions Architect**.
- **Accessible Exports:** PDF briefings are strictly styled for WCAG 2.2 compliance (high-contrast, B&W).

## Accessibility Features
- **Color Contrast:** Themes are optimized for WCAG AA compliance.
- **Navigation:** Full keyboard support with visible focus rings (`Tab` to navigate).
- **Screen Readers:** Comprehensive ARIA labels and semantic HTML structure.
- **High-Contrast Print:** PDF exports are automatically converted to pure high-contrast B&W for maximum readability.

## Getting Started
1. **Onboarding:** Follow the Career Hub briefing on your first launch.
2. **Setup Trajectory:** Head to the Architect Mode to define your first major ambition (e.g., AWS Solutions Architect).
3. **Daily Scheduler:** Check your Daily Scheduler every morning to sync your tactical objectives.
4. **Transmission:** Generate a Weekly or Monthly Transmission to reconcile your progress and check trajectory alignment.
